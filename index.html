About MMCB

Host. Relax. Celebrate. All in One Place

M'M Commercial Building is your ultimate destination for unforgettable events, leisure, and relaxation. We seamlessly combine a modern Function Hall and the vibrant Top-Floor Lounge & Pool, offering the strategic advantage of two premium venues in one building. This unique setup allows for effortless transitions between spaces, accommodating a wide range of occasions—from intimate private parties to large corporate gatherings.

Our Function Hall features state-of-the-art lighting, sound systems, and flexible seating arrangements, perfect for weddings, seminars, conferences, and social celebrations. Meanwhile, the Top-Floor Lounge & Pool provides a chic, vibrant atmosphere for evening receptions, rooftop parties, or casual relaxation, all with panoramic city views that create a memorable backdrop for every occasion.

Whether you’re planning a sophisticated event or seeking a relaxing escape, M'M Commercial Building delivers a seamless blend of elegance, comfort, and convenience. With premium amenities, modern interiors, ample parking, and professional event coordination, we ensure every detail is thoughtfully curated. Host, relax, and celebrate—all in one place, where every experience is reliable, high-value, and unforgettable.
